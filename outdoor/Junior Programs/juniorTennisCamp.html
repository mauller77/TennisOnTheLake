Half Day Tennis Camp
Cost: $299
AM Camp 9:00 am to 12:00 pm
PM Camp 12:00 pm to 3:00 pm
The morning or afternoon half-day camps consist of approximately 90 minutes warm-up and stroke development (drilling technique for ground strokes, volleys and serve), followed by a 15-20 minute snack break at “The Trailer”. The remaining time is spent playing a variety of level-specific games that help students to further develop their skills in a fun, competitive scenario.
Full Day Tennis Camp
Cost: $499
9:00 AM to 3:00 PM
Camp format will be similar to the half-day sessions. Additionally, full-day students will have 45-minute lunch break after their morning session and, with a typically lower student to coach ratio, will have more opportunities in the afternoon to work on stroke consistency, rallying and point play. Students will have an opportunity to play in a real tournament at the end of the week.
We recommend full-day kids tennis camp for players age 8 & up.
High School Tennis Camp
Cost: $299
12:00 PM to 3:00 PM
Specifically designed for campers age 13-17. Whether you are trying to make your junior high or high school team or trying to make it to number one, this is the camp for you! Campers are grouped according to age and skill level with opportunities to move up in the group through competition. While we still work on technical proficiency, more emphasis is spent on match play tactics and strategy. Students will have an opportunity to play in a real tournament at the end of the week.
