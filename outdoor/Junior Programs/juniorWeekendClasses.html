Junior Weekend Classes
Students are grouped according to age and skill level. Students with sufficient skills may move up to an older age group. Classes are split between drills that work on proper stroke technique and age/skill-appropriate game play to develop court awareness and good sportsmanship. USTA Quickstart® methods (slower balls, shorter nets and smaller courts) are used for 10 & Under classes making it easier for students to quickly succeed, gain confidence and have FUN!
