Private Programs
Have a particular stroke or strategy you’d like to work on or a group of friends you’d like to play with exclusively? Then private programs are for you! Work with our coaches to develop a customized training regimen or regular hitting session at days and times convenient for you.
Coach assignments and private class schedules can remain consistent for the time frame of each four-week session. However, coach and court availability may vary somewhat from session to session and some coach substitutions may be necessary. Scheduled privates require 24-hour notice of cancellation or are subject to a 50% charge.
Individual privates and packages can be purchased below. Receive $10 off per lesson by purchasing a 10-pack. Please call or email to schedule your coach and court time.
